ABOUT INTEL ONEAPI COMMUNITY

Welcome to our Intel Communities! This is a place to ask questions and share knowledge about Intel products and solutions. Interested in learning more about Cloud technologies, 5G, or Gaming, for example? You can chat with an expert in our Blogs geared to topics important for our customers. Or if you have a product support question, we have support forums you can visit. Some forums offer peer-to-peer only support while others off support from Intel Product Support Engineers. We are glad you are here!

Overview

This program is focused on undergraduate and graduate students who are passionate about technology and working

with developer communities to promote learning, sharing, and collaboration.

It provides opportunities for students to enhance their oneAPI skills,

expand their network, and learn about the cutting-edge Intel® hardware and software products.

Why IOSC?

  1. Recognition from Intel as a oneAPI expert
  2. Support from Intel to organize local events, such as watch parties and workshops
  3. Extended access to Intel® Developer Cloud
  4. Early access to the latest technology developments that are under a nondisclosure agreement (NDA)
  5. Invitations to exclusive Intel events and training
  6. Reimbursement from Intel for participating in accepted conferences and events
  7. Opportunity to be considered for an internship at Intel
